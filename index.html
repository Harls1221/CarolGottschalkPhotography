On my 10th birthday my mother gave me a digital camera, hung up a sheet and handed my friends and I a stack of her dresses. We spent hours playing dress-up and taking photos and from that day on I have had a deep love for photography. As I have grown into an adult it is a passion that has continued to grow. From childhood games to now capturing life's most special moments to cherish forever.

I believe photos are a view into our past, when we're old and gray and memories have began to fade, we hold dearly onto those moments we were able to freeze in time and cherish the emotions caught in a photo.
